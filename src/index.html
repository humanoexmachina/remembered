Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Remembered free to use?

    Remembered is currently free to use in its limited beta phase. In the future, we will adpot a paid model to support ongoing product development. However, we remain committed to never including ads or selling your data for profit as our number one value is: your privacy comes first!

  • What desktop or mobile devices can I use Remembered on?

    Remembered is a desktop app that you can use on both Windows and Mac computers.

  • Where are my digital memories stored?

    Your messages are stored locally on your desktop computer within the application. All photos, videos, audio and other multimedia files are stored in your home folder, in the "Remembered" subfolder.

    To find your media files, navigate to the following folder:

    Mac: ~/Remembered

  • Do other people have access to my private chat data?

    No one except for you has access to your private data.

  • How long will my digital memories be stored?

    Forever on your computer hard drive.

  • Can Remembered merge chats from different apps?

    Yes - Remembered automatically merge your chats with the same person or group across different apps so you can see all your chats in one place.

  • How do I uninstall Remembered and delete my data?

    Simply go through the standard uninstall process for desktop apps on Mac or Windows. Remembered won't take up any disk space after uninstallation.
