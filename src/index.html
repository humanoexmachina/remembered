Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Remembered free to use?

    Remembered is currently in Alpha and is 100% free to use. In the future, Remembered will be a paid app, but we will never put ads in the app or sell your data to make money. The new pricing model will help us support the development of the software without sacrificing our values for respecting the privacy of our users.

  • What desktop or mobile devices can I use Remembered on?

    Remembered is a desktop app that you can use on both Windows and Mac computers.

  • Where are my messages stored?

    Your messages are stored locally on your desktop computer within the application. All photos, videos, audio messages and other medias such as file attachments are stored in your home folder, in the "Remembered" subfolder.

    To find your media files, navigate to the following folder:

    Windows: ???

    Mac: ~/Remembered

    No one except you can read your messages.
